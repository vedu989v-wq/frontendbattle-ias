Frequently Asked Questions

What courses do you offer for UPSC? +
We offer GS Foundation, Advanced GS, Mains Answer Writing, and Optional Subject courses. You can also enrol in our UPSC Mentorship Program for personalised one-to-one guidance throughout your preparation.
Are there online coaching options? +
Yes, we provide full-fledged online coaching with live classes, recorded lectures, and digital study material. You can attend from anywhere in India with a stable internet connection.
How long are the batches? +
GS Foundation batches run for 12 months, while short-term crash courses are 3–6 months depending on the exam cycle. Weekend batches are available for working professionals.
Can I attend a demo class before enrolling? +
Yes! We organise free demo classes every month. You can register on our Courses page or contact us directly via phone or the contact form on our website.
